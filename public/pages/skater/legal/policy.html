Skater Policy

Applies to all skaters using Roll Show

1. Overview

This Skater Policy explains how skaters are expected to use Roll Show. It applies to any skater account, profile, show, appearance, or content you create or participate in on the platform.

2. Skater Accounts and Profiles

  • You must provide accurate information in your skater profile (name, stage name, discipline, etc.).
  • You are responsible for all activity under your account.
  • You must keep your login credentials secure and not share them with others.

3. Safety and Conduct

  • You are responsible for your own physical safety during any skating activity.
  • You must follow local laws, venue rules, and safety guidelines at all times.
  • You may not engage in harassment, hate, threats, or abusive behavior on or off the platform in connection with Roll Show.
  • You may not promote dangerous stunts without appropriate context, safety, and legality.

4. Shows, Events, and Appearances

  • Show details you publish (time, location, requirements) must be accurate to the best of your knowledge.
  • You are responsible for arriving prepared and on time for any confirmed appearance or show.
  • Cancellation or no‑show behavior may affect your visibility or access to future opportunities.
  • Roll Show may display your profile, shows, and related content to buyers, businesses, and other users.

5. Content and Rights

  • You should only upload or submit content you have the right to use (video, images, music, logos, etc.).
  • You remain the owner of your original content.
  • By using the platform, you allow Roll Show to host, display, and transmit your content as needed to operate the service.
  • Some opportunities may involve additional terms (for example, specific show or brand deals); those will be presented separately when applicable.

6. Payments and Opportunities

  • Any payouts, splits, or fees are handled through the platform’s payment systems and applicable role‑specific rules.
  • Roll Show does not guarantee any minimum number of bookings, views, or income.
  • Disputes about payments may result in temporary holds while we review activity.

7. Prohibited Behavior

  • Fraud, misrepresentation, or attempting to scam other users.
  • Sharing private information of others without consent.
  • Using Roll Show to promote illegal activity.
  • Attempting to bypass platform systems, fees, or safety checks.

8. Moderation and Enforcement

  • Roll Show may remove content, limit visibility, or suspend accounts that violate this policy or the Terms of Use.
  • Serious or repeated violations may result in permanent removal from the platform.
  • We may cooperate with venues, businesses, or authorities when necessary.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Skater Policy as the platform evolves. Continued use of a skater account after changes means you accept the updated policy.
