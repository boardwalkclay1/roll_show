Musician Policy

Applies to all musicians using Roll Show

1. Overview

This Musician Policy explains how musicians and artists are expected to use Roll Show. It applies to any musician account, profile, track, performance, or content you create or share on the platform.

2. Musician Accounts and Profiles

  • You must provide accurate information in your musician profile (name, stage name, genre, etc.).
  • You are responsible for all activity under your account.
  • You must keep your login credentials secure and not share them with others.

3. Content Ownership and Rights

  • You should only upload or submit music and media you have the right to use.
  • You remain the owner of your original music and content.
  • By using the platform, you allow Roll Show to host, stream, display, and transmit your content as needed to operate the service.
  • If your content involves third‑party rights (labels, publishers, collaborators), you are responsible for honoring those obligations.

4. Performances and Shows

  • Show and performance details you publish must be accurate to the best of your knowledge.
  • You are responsible for being prepared and on time for any confirmed performance.
  • Cancellation or no‑show behavior may affect your visibility or access to future opportunities.

5. Payments and Monetization

  • Any payouts, splits, or fees are handled through the platform’s payment systems and applicable role‑specific rules.
  • Roll Show does not guarantee any minimum number of plays, bookings, or income.
  • Disputes about payments may result in temporary holds while we review activity.

6. Conduct and Community

  • You may not use Roll Show to harass, threaten, or abuse others.
  • You may not upload content that is unlawful, hateful, or intentionally harmful.
  • You may not use Roll Show to promote fraud, scams, or misleading offers.

7. Prohibited Uses

  • Uploading content you do not have rights to distribute.
  • Attempting to artificially inflate plays, views, or engagement.
  • Bypassing platform fees or systems in ways that violate our Terms of Use.

8. Moderation and Enforcement

  • Roll Show may remove content, limit visibility, or suspend accounts that violate this policy or the Terms of Use.
  • Serious or repeated violations may result in permanent removal from the platform.
  • We may cooperate with rights holders or authorities when necessary.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Musician Policy as the platform evolves. Continued use of a musician account after changes means you accept the updated policy.
