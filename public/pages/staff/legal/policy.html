Staff & Admin Policy

Applies to internal staff and admin users of Roll Show

1. Overview

This Staff & Admin Policy applies to anyone with elevated access to Roll Show systems, including moderators, support staff, and administrators. It governs how internal tools and user data may be accessed and used.

2. Access and Accounts

  • Staff accounts are personal and must not be shared.
  • Access is granted only as needed for your role and responsibilities.
  • You must use strong, unique passwords and follow any additional security requirements.

3. Use of User Data

  • User data may only be accessed for legitimate operational reasons (support, moderation, security, etc.).
  • You may not browse user data out of curiosity or for personal reasons.
  • You may not copy, export, or share user data outside approved systems.

4. Moderation and Enforcement Actions

  • Any action taken on a user account (warnings, suspensions, removals) must follow internal guidelines.
  • Decisions should be documented where required by internal processes.
  • Abuse of moderation tools is strictly prohibited.

5. Confidentiality

  • Internal tools, dashboards, and processes are confidential.
  • You may not disclose internal systems, vulnerabilities, or private information to unauthorized parties.

6. Security Responsibilities

  • You must report suspected security issues or data incidents immediately through the appropriate channel.
  • You may not attempt to bypass security controls or test systems without authorization.

7. Prohibited Behavior

  • Using staff access to target, harass, or retaliate against users.
  • Accessing accounts or data unrelated to your work.
  • Sharing internal tools or credentials with anyone else.

8. Consequences

  • Violations of this policy may result in loss of access, removal from staff roles, or further action.
  • Serious violations may be escalated to legal or external authorities if necessary.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Staff & Admin Policy as the platform evolves. Continued use of staff or admin access after changes means you accept the updated policy.
