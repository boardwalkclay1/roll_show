Business Policy

Applies to all businesses using Roll Show

1. Overview

This Business Policy explains how businesses, venues, brands, and sponsors are expected to use Roll Show. It applies to any business account, profile, submission, sponsorship, or campaign you create on the platform.

2. Business Accounts and Verification

  • You must provide accurate business information (company name, contact details, country, etc.).
  • We may require verification before enabling certain features or payouts.
  • You are responsible for all activity under your business account.

3. Submissions and Campaigns

  • You may submit offers, events, ads, venues, sponsorships, and other proposals through the platform.
  • All submissions must be accurate, lawful, and not misleading.
  • We may review, approve, reject, or remove submissions at our discretion.

4. Advertising and Sponsorship

  • Ads and sponsorships must comply with applicable advertising laws and guidelines.
  • You may not submit content that is deceptive, hateful, or harmful.
  • We may label or present sponsored content in ways that are clear to users.

5. Payments and Payouts

  • Payouts to businesses are handled through the platform’s payment systems.
  • Fees, revenue splits, and payout schedules may be shown in your dashboard or related documentation.
  • We may hold or delay payouts if we detect unusual or potentially fraudulent activity.

6. Use of Skater and Musician Content

  • Any use of skater or musician content beyond what the platform normally provides may require separate arrangements.
  • You may not download, reuse, or republish user content outside the platform without appropriate rights.

7. Conduct and Compliance

  • You must comply with local laws, venue rules, and safety requirements for any events or promotions.
  • You may not use Roll Show to run scams, fake promotions, or misleading offers.
  • You may not harass or pressure users into off‑platform deals that violate our Terms of Use.

8. Moderation and Enforcement

  • Roll Show may remove business submissions, limit visibility, or suspend accounts that violate this policy or the Terms of Use.
  • Serious or repeated violations may result in permanent removal from the platform.
  • We may cooperate with payment providers, venues, or authorities when necessary.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Business Policy as the platform evolves. Continued use of a business account after changes means you accept the updated policy.
