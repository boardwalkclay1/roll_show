Privacy Policy

Applies to all Roll Show users and visitors

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Roll Show collects, uses, and protects information across the entire platform. By using Roll Show, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

This includes:

  • Name, email, password
  • Role (skater, musician, buyer, business, staff)
  • Profile details (stage name, discipline, company info, display name)
  • Uploaded content (images, videos, shows, offers, submissions)

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device and browser information
  • IP address and general location
  • Usage data (pages visited, actions taken)
  • Security logs and diagnostics

C. Information From Platform Activity

  • Ticket purchases and wallet activity
  • Show hosting, bookings, submissions, and offers
  • Messages and interactions with other users

3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Operate and improve the platform
  • Deliver shows, bookings, and ticketing features
  • Process payments and prevent fraud
  • Verify business accounts and submissions
  • Personalize your experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Sharing of Information

We may share information with:

  • Service providers (payments, hosting, analytics)
  • Other users when required for platform features
  • Businesses you interact with
  • Authorities when required by law

5. Data Security

We use encryption, secure communication, and restricted access to protect your data. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to safeguard your information.

6. Data Retention

We retain information as long as necessary to operate the platform, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Update your account information
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Request access to the data we store about you

Some data may be retained for legal, security, or operational reasons even after account deletion.

8. Children

Roll Show is not intended for children under the age where they can legally consent to data processing in their jurisdiction.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the platform evolves. Continued use of Roll Show after changes means you accept the updated policy.
