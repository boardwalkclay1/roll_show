Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Roll Show collects, uses, stores, and protects information across the entire platform. This includes all user types: skaters, musicians, buyers, businesses, staff, and visitors.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in three categories:

A. Information You Provide

This includes account details and profile information such as:

  • Name, email, password
  • Role (skater, musician, buyer, business, etc.)
  • Profile details (stage name, discipline, company info, display name)
  • Uploaded content (images, videos, descriptions, show listings, offers)

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information (browser, OS, IP address)
  • Usage data (pages visited, actions taken, timestamps)
  • Log data for security and diagnostics

C. Information From Platform Activity

  • Ticket purchases and wallet activity
  • Show hosting, bookings, submissions, and offers
  • Messages, comments, and interactions with other users

3. How We Use Information

Roll Show uses your information to:

  • Operate and improve the platform
  • Deliver shows, bookings, and ticketing features
  • Process payments and prevent fraud
  • Verify business accounts and submissions
  • Personalize your experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Sharing of Information

We may share information with:

  • Service providers (payment processors, hosting, analytics)
  • Other users when necessary for platform features (e.g., show listings, profiles)
  • Businesses you interact with (e.g., show hosts, sponsors)
  • Law enforcement or authorities when required by law

5. Data Security

We use encryption, secure communication, and restricted access to protect your data. No system is completely secure, but we take reasonable measures to safeguard your information.

6. Data Retention

We retain information as long as necessary to operate the platform, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Update your account information
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Request access to the data we store about you

Some data may be retained for legal, security, or operational reasons even after account deletion.

8. Children

Roll Show is not intended for children under the age where they can legally consent to data processing in their jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the platform evolves. Continued use of Roll Show after changes means you accept the updated policy.
