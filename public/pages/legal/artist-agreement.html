Artist Agreement

1. Parties

This Artist Agreement (“Agreement”) is between you (“Artist”) and Roll Show (“Platform”).

2. Purpose

The Platform provides tools for Artists to upload, manage, and license music for use in shows, performances, and other content.

3. Grant of Rights

Artist grants the Platform a non-exclusive, worldwide license to host, stream, transmit, and display the Artist’s audio content as necessary to operate the Platform and to enable licensed uses by approved counterparties (e.g., skaters, businesses) under separate deals.

4. Ownership

Artist retains ownership of all rights in their music, subject to the licenses granted in this Agreement and any specific deal terms agreed with counterparties.

5. Licensing to Skaters and Businesses

Artist may authorize use of specific tracks by skaters or businesses through offers, deals, or in-app licensing flows. Each such deal may specify territory, duration, media, and compensation. The Platform may record and enforce these terms.

6. Royalties and Payments

Where applicable, Artist will receive royalties or fees as defined in specific deals or Platform programs. The Platform may deduct processing or platform fees as disclosed in the Artist dashboard or deal terms.

7. Representations and Warranties

Artist represents that they own or control all rights necessary to grant the licenses in this Agreement and that their content does not infringe third-party rights.

8. Content Standards

Artist agrees not to upload content that is unlawful, abusive, or otherwise violates Platform policies.

9. Term and Termination

This Agreement remains in effect while Artist uses the Platform. Either party may terminate it, but existing licenses and deals may continue as specified in their terms.

10. Limitation of Liability

The Platform’s liability is limited as described in the Terms of Use.
